The complete 15-lesson text curriculum, model wording, drills, troubleshooting, ethics, quizzes, bookmarks and progress tracking are implemented. This file defines the remaining original media and release assets.

## Recording standard

- Video: 1080p, 30 fps, landscape master; capture overhead inserts for paper and object handling.
- Audio: clean lavalier or boom recording with no copyrighted background music.
- Participants must know the recording is an entertainment demonstration and sign a model release.
- Avoid collecting or displaying private information, grief-related material, health questions, passwords or financial details.
- Commercial routines and gimmicks may not be reconstructed, exposed or copied.

## Content and attribution rules

All scripts, diagrams, illustrations and routine combinations must be original. Historical principles should be credited where known. Use books and legitimate purchased instruction for research, but do not copy passages, commercial scripts, proprietary gimmick construction, marked systems, book-test layouts or branded routines.

A bibliography may include established literature such as Corinda, Annemann, Ian Rowland, Bob Cassidy, Banachek and broader memory literature, but inclusion in the bibliography does not authorise copying their expression or proprietary material.

## Release acceptance criteria

- All lessons, search, bookmarks, completion and quizzes work offline.
- No participant’s private writing or personal data appears in release media.
- All artwork and music are original, licensed or public domain with records retained.
- Every lesson involving deception contains a clear entertainment and ethics boundary.
- Three external test users can follow the practice procedure without verbal clarification.
- Android production build installs, resumes and preserves progress on a physical low-memory device.
